# Road Accident & Crime Risk Analysis (2022)

## **Project Overview**
This project analyzes crime and road accident data across Indian states/UTs for the year 2022 to assess the **risk level** of each state. The risk score is calculated based on normalized values of reported crimes, total victims, road accidents, and fatalities. States are classified into **Low, Medium, and High Risk**.

---

## **Objective**
- Identify high-risk states based on combined crime and road accident data.
- Provide visual insights through charts for better understanding.
- Generate a clean dataset for reporting and further analysis.
